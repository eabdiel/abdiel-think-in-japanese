Getting Started
Use the portal as a guided study path: • Learn phrases in Parts 1–3 • Study characters in Reading Aid and Frequency Deck • Practice in Kana Dojo and Kanji Hub • Follow the 90-Day Tracker and Study Plan • Reduce romaji over time. Small daily practice is more effective than occasional marathon sessions.

Think in Japanese: Character Reading Aid, Part 4

A deduped writing and reading aid built only from the 300 phrases in Parts 1–3. Sorted by romaji sound, with character type, frequency, and an example phrase from your own study sheets.

Important: Hiragana and katakana sounds are stable here. Kanji readings are context-based from your 300 phrases, so a kanji may have more than one romaji reading when it appeared in different words. This keeps the sheet practical for reading your existing phrases instead of becoming a full dictionary.
