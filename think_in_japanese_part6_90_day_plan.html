Think in Japanese – Part 6

90-Day Japanese Brain Rewiring Plan

Built from Parts 1–5. Focused on thinking in Japanese, not passing exams.

Mission

Your goal is not memorizing grammar rules. Your goal is to replace common internal thoughts with Japanese so the language becomes part of your daily thinking process.

Phase 1 – Survival Brain

Days 1–14

  • 25 phrases
  • Top 25 frequency characters
  • Tired, hungry, thirsty, confusion, basic reactions

Examples: 疲れた ・ お腹すいた ・ 何これ？ ・ どうしよう

Goal: Start thinking basic needs in Japanese.

Phase 2 – Daily Life Narration

Days 15–30

  • 50 phrases total
  • Top 50 frequency characters
  • Work, home, travel, decisions

Examples: 今何時？ ・ 後でやろう ・ 忘れた ・ まだ早い

Goal: Narrate your day in Japanese.

Phase 3 – Social Survival

Days 31–45

  • 75 phrases total
  • Questions and clarifications

Examples: 何て言うの？ ・ どういう意味？ ・ 本当？ ・ なるほど

Goal: Think what you would actually say to another person.

Phase 4 – Puerto Rican Mode (Osaka Mode)

Days 46–60

  • 100 phrases total
  • Kansai / Osaka casual speech

Examples: なんでやねん ・ ほんま？ ・ あかん ・ めっちゃ ・ おもろい

Goal: Understand casual native reactions and humor.

Phase 5 – Gaki No Tsukai Mode

Days 61–75

  • 150 phrases total
  • Reaction and comedy vocabulary

Examples: ありえへん ・ うそやろ ・ 何してんねん ・ 今のアウトやろ

Goal: Follow Japanese variety show reactions naturally.

Phase 6 – Reading Unlock

Days 76–90

  • Remove romaji
  • Read Japanese directly
  • Top 100 characters recognized instantly

Goal: Recognize words without sounding them out first.

Success Criteria After 90 Days

  • 150+ phrases active in memory
  • 100 most common characters recognized
  • No romaji dependency
  • Can understand many reactions in Japanese variety shows
  • Can naturally think simple thoughts in Japanese
