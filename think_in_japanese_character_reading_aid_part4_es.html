Piensa en Japonés: Ayuda de lectura de caracteres, Partee 4

A deduped writing and reading aid built only from the 300 phrases in Partes 1–3. Sorted by romaji sound, with character type, frequency, and an example phrase from your own study sheets.

Important: Hiragana and katakana sounds are stable here. Kanji readings are context-based from your 300 phrases, so a kanji may have more than one romaji reading when it appeared in different words. This keeps the sheet practical for reading your existing phrases instead of becoming a full dictionary.
